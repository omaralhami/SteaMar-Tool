Frequently Asked Questions

What's new in this version? ▼
Improved performance, better UI, faster game loading, and automatic updates.
How fast is it compared to previous versions? ▼
Optimized for speed with instant game adding and faster downloads.
What are the requirements? ▼
Windows 10 or 11 (64-bit), 4GB RAM minimum, and Steam installed.
Is auto-updating safe? Does it work? ▼
Yes, auto-updating is safe and fully functional. Updates are checked on startup.
Is my data safe and private? ▼
Yes. All data is stored locally on your computer. Your information remains private.
