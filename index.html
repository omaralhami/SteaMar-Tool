SteaMar Tool

Manage Steam manifests in one click.

Add Steam games, pull manifests, grab Lua activation scripts, and browse online fixes in one place.

How does it work?▼
Add games to Steam, download and manage manifests, run built-in Lua activations, and browse online fixes, all in one tool.
How fast is it?▼
Manifest pulls and game adds are optimized with cached sessions, so most actions complete in a click.
Requirements?▼
Windows 10/11 (64-bit) with Steam and Discord installed and signed in.
Is it safe?▼
Yes. Manifests and Lua come from the official GitHub, applied locally. Your Steam login isn’t stored, and everything stays on your PC. Discord is our support.
